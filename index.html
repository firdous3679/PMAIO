Precision Medicine with AI in Oncology

Innovative Approaches to Cancer Detection and Treatment

About the Book

"Precision Medicine with AI in Oncology - Innovative Approaches to Cancer Detection and Treatment" is an upcoming book seeking contributions from researchers, clinicians, and experts in the field of artificial intelligence and oncology.

This book aims to provide a comprehensive exploration of how AI is reshaping cancer detection and treatment, covering fundamental concepts, applications in early detection, predictive models, personalized treatment, ethical considerations, and real-world case studies.

The book is edited by Dr. Danish Jamil, Dr. Shehnilla Zardari, Dr. Firdous Kausar, and Dr. Khalid Hussain and will be published by Springer Nature Switzerland AG.

Important Dates:

  • Abstract Submission: 31 March 2024
  • Full Chapter Submission: 15 July 2024
  • Submission to Publisher: January 2025
  • Estimated Published Date: September 2024

Publication Charge: No APC or Publication Charge.

Scope for Publication

The book aims to provide a comprehensive exploration of the transformative role of artificial intelligence (AI) in the field of oncology, with a focus on precision medicine. We invite contributions that address fundamental concepts, cutting-edge applications, and ethical considerations related to the integration of AI technologies in cancer detection and treatment.

Submission Guidelines & Review Process

Submission Guidelines

Send your 500-word abstract by the designated deadline. We are looking for 6000-10000 word chapters. Make sure that the list of co-authors is provided with Google Scholar and Orcid profiles alongside the abstracts.

Review Process

All chapters will go through the Turnitin plagiarism test and papers with an overall similarity of less than 15% will be considered for acceptance by the editorial team for the book.
